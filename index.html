LMSC is a premier science and mathematics club dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and excellence in STEM education. We organize competitions, workshops, and events that challenge students to push their boundaries and explore the fascinating worlds of science and mathematics.

Achievements on the International Stages

3rd International Olympiad of Astronomy & Astrophysics, Nepal, 2024

Congratulations to Farhan Sajid for his great achievement in the 3rd International Olympiad of Astronomy & Astrophysics junior held in Nepal. He is the pride of our school, club and our nation. We are very proud to have him as upcoming president co-ordinator in LMSC.

March 2024

The 18th International Olympiad on Astronomy and Astrophysics, 2025

Tasdik Ahmed won a Silver medal for our country at The 18th International Olympiad on Astronomy and Astrophysics (IOAA) was held in Mumbai, India, from August 11-21, 2025, organized by the Homi Bhabha Centre for Science Education (HBCSE).

September 2023

35th International Biology Olympiad (IBO)

Fayez Ahmed, secured one bronze medal for our country at The 35th International Biology Olympiad (IBO) was held in Astana, Kazakhstan, from July 7 to July 14, 2024, featuring 290 students from 75 countries. He is a proud of our school , nation and club as well.

December 2023
